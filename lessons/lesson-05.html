Current Measurement (A / mA)

Current is measured in series — you must break the circuit and insert the meter inline. Getting this wrong blows the fuse or damages the circuit.

mA
Milliamps
Small currents: LEDs, sensors, MCU pins. Red → mAVΩ port.
200µA / 2mA / 20mA / 200mA
10A
Amps (High)
Motors, car batteries, appliances. Move red to 10A port. 30-second limit.
Max 10A — dedicated port
⚡
Critical: Current MUST be measured in series. Connecting in parallel creates a near-short that blows the fuse instantly and can damage the meter or cause a fire.

Example — LED Current Draw
Series Circuit
  1. Turn off circuit. Set to mA DC. Red probe → mAVΩ port.
  2. Disconnect one wire from the LED to break the series path.
  3. Insert meter: red to wire from +, black to component.
  4. Power on and read.
